Kora is an open-source, beautifully designed, behavioral Android launcher. It is built to help users manage their screen time by introducing friction before opening distracting applications.

Features

* Glassmorphic UI: Premium, dark-themed, glassmorphic design that doesn't obscure your wallpaper.
* Rising Tide Interception: Set application limits. When opening flagged applications, Kora intercepts you to ask why you are opening the app.
* Smart App Drawer: Floating search bar, categorized flagged apps, and usage statistics.
* To-Do Hub: Integrated task manager with daily intentions, progress tracking, and history.
* Kora Hub: Centralized dashboard to manage AI models, device permissions, and launcher settings.

Privacy
Kora respects your privacy. All usage statistics are kept strictly on your device. The upcoming offline AI integration runs 100% locally with no internet connection required.
